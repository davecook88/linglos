Welcome to linglos

Linglos is a tool which has been developed to help you to learn words in-depth - not just superficially.

Too many language learners simply study pairs of words with a translation in their native language and never successfully grasp how to use the word. Linglos is a fun way to improve this depth of knowledge and improve understanding.
